01 // ABOUT

We Were Never Meant To Stop Here

Space Junkies Uganda is East Africa's most passionate space enthusiast community. Launched in January 2025 by Ronnie Atuhaire, we bring together astronomers, rocket dreamers, sci-fi lovers, astrophotographers, and anyone who looks up and feels the pull.

We're open to absolutely everyone — no degrees required, no equipment needed. Just wonder, curiosity, and a willingness to look at the night sky differently.

RONNIE ATUHAIRE
FOUNDER & MISSION COMMANDER // SPACE JUNKIES UGANDA

"We started this because Ugandans deserve to be part of the space story — not just as spectators, but as dreamers, builders, and explorers."

ABOUT SPACE JUNKIES UGANDA

Founded in January 2025 by Ronnie Atuhaire, Space Junkies Uganda is East Africa's premier space enthusiast community. We're 135 members strong and growing — united by our passion for the cosmos. From stargazing nights at Kololo Hill to dark sky expeditions at Lake Mburo, we explore the universe together. Uganda's unique equatorial position (0.3136°N 32.5811°E) gives us spectacular views of both Northern and Southern hemisphere constellations. We're dreamers, scientists, photographers, and explorers — all welcome, no experience required.
